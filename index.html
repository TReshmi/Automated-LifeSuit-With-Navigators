About Our Project

Automated Life Suit with Navigators is an innovative life-saving solution designed to assist people in flood-prone areas by automatically transporting them from disaster sites to the nearest shore. Our system integrates cutting-edge technology, including a DC motor, LED indicators, a lead acid battery, an Arduino board, a GSM module, and Bluetooth modules, ensuring efficient navigation and communication during emergencies.


Developed by Team Hustlers, this project aims to revolutionize disaster response and provide a safer, more reliable way to save lives during floods. By leveraging automation and real-time tracking, our life suit enhances survival chances, reducing the risks faced by individuals stranded in rising waters.


We are dedicated to refining this system further and are seeking support to bring this vision to life. Join us in making a difference!

Motorized Propulsion

DC motors and a propeller system enable effortless movement, reducing physical strain in emergencies.

Real-Time GPS Tracking

Integrated GPS provides precise location tracking for quick and efficient rescue.

Advanced Health Monitoring

Tracks heart rate and oxygen levels to ensure timely medical attention.

Self-Sustaining Suit

Designed for independent operation, ensuring functionality without external assistance during emergencies.
